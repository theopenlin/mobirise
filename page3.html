Mega

Mega is a cloud storage and file hosting service offered by Mega Limited, an Auckland-based company. The service is offered primarily through web-based apps. Mega mobile apps are also available for Windows Phone, Android and iOS. Mega is known for its security feature where all files are end-to-end encrypted locally before they are uploaded. - Wikipedia
